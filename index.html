So basically Santa Laurensia School (MY school) went on an Educational Trip!!! I chose to go to Taiwan and we did some fun stuff but it is an educational trip so like, we have work now :(

What we did!!!

So we went there by plane for like 5 hours and we met our tour guide/teacher on the bus!! We settled in the hotel for a night and I'm going to be honest, I didn't sleep that night. Then the next day we went to the Holy Family Catholic Church. We took notes about the church and stuff then we moved to Yilan and plucked onions. It was there when my phone broke because I was washing the spring onions and I was wearing my phone on a necklace-like hook and it dipped into the onion water. After that we settled into our new hotel in Hualien !!! I didn't sleep that night either (I think). Then we spent the next 3 days in the Stella Maris Ursuline Highschool, getting to know the students and stuff. On the first of those 3 days, we visited the school and then the Tzu Chi Foundation Resource Centre and learned a ton about its history. Then for dinner, we went to the DongDaMen Night Market.

This is the Tzu Chi Foundation Resource Centre!!

On the 2nd day (of those 3 days), we did some charity work at the Tzu Chi Foundation Resource Centre. Me, Michael, and Clara took apart like 10 bicycle wheels. Then after that, we went back to the school and spent some time with the students. After that, we went to the Amis Mochi Factory and made some mochis. I'm not going to lie, the mochis did not taste very good...

This is me in the mochi factory, not a very good image but I have to make do with what Audrey took cuz my phone died.

On the last of those 3 days, we spent one last day with the Stella Maris students by doing some fun experiments and cooking dumplings!! We then transferred to Taipei by bus and visited the Chiang Kai Shek Memorial Hall. After that, we went to the XiMenDing Night Market by the Taipei MRT and walking. We checked into the hotel and I was very happy because all 4 buses had to share the same hotel, which meant being able to see my boyfriend!!! (He was in Kaohsiung)

This is me and Michael in CoCo Curry in XiMenDing. (Audrey took the photo)

The next day, Hualien visited the Yehliu Geopark. It was an absolute disaster. So when we were driving there, it was raining hard, but when we arrived, it was barely drizzling, so I thought my umbrella would keep me safe. *loud incorrect buzzer*

Everything was okay at first but then the rain got harder and harder until it was basically Noah's Arc. Seriously, the wind and rain got so strong I felt like my fatahh was going to fly away.
